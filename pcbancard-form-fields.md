# PC Bancard Application Form Fields

## Contact Information
- **PCBancard Representative**: Randy Sinclair
- **Phone**: 682-218-1669

## Form Fields

### Business Information
1. **BUSINESS NAME** (text input)
2. **DBA NAME** (text input - "Doing Business As")
3. **ADDRESS** (text input - full business address)
4. **BUSINESS #** (text input - business phone number)

### Contact Information
5. **OWNER** (text input - owner name)
6. **CELL#** (text input - owner cell phone)
7. **MANAGER** (text input - manager name)
8. **CELL#** (text input - manager cell phone)

### Operational Details
9. **HOURS OF OPERATION** (text input - e.g., "9 AM - 6 PM")
10. **DAYS OF OPERATION** (text input - e.g., "Monday - Friday")
11. **EST. MONTHLY VOLUME** (number input - estimated monthly transaction volume)

### Additional Information
12. **SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS** (textarea - any special notes or requirements)

## Required Document Uploads

1. **Driver's License** (image/pdf upload)
   - Copy of business owner's driver's license

2. **Voided Check** (image/pdf upload)
   - Must match bank account on application
   - Shows account number and routing number

3. **State EIN #** (image/pdf upload)
   - Copy of state Employer Identification Number

4. **Business Address Verification** (image/pdf upload)
   - Document proving business address matches application

5. **Bank Letter** (image/pdf upload)
   - Letter from bank stating account is in good standing
   - Must be in company's name
   - Must show account # and routing # matching application and voided check

## Processing Information
- **Processing Time**: 2-3 business days
- All documents must be uploaded before submission
- Business address on application must match verification documents
- Bank account information must match across all documents (voided check, bank letter, application)
